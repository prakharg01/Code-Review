## Pinout


### SPI : SPI3
|Function | GPIO | Pin |
| :---: | :---: | :---: |
|MOSI | PC12 | CN7-3 |
|MISO | PC11 | CN7-2 |
|SCK  | PC10 | CN7-1 |
|NSS  | PA6  | CN7-32 |

Note: Do not forget to connect the ground on the 
SPI master (RPi) and the SPI slave device (STM Nucleo).
